A new way to pay

We've rolled out new purchase functionality using Battle.net Balance that adds an extra bit of convenience to your game shopping experience. When you shop on Battle.net, you can now pay using a combination of Battle.net Balance and other payment methods*.

For example, if you have a little bit of Battle.net Balance left over, you'll now be able to use that to pay for part of a purchase. If your Battle.net Balance doesn’t cover the full cost, you’ll be able to pay the remainder directly at checkout using yourchosen payment method.

Try it out now!

*The new method works for all purchases if your Balance Currency matches the Product Currency. This method is disabled for some payment methods with long expiration times.
